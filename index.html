About

Built on Craftsmanship, Reliability, and a Great Eye for Detail

Good finish carpentry is supposed to feel like it belongs there. The lines are clean, the reveals stay consistent, and the final details make the whole room feel finished.

That is the standard Eagle Ridge Finish Carpentry, LLC has carried for 40 years. The work centers on careful layout, solid fit, steady hands, and an honest respect for the home, whether the project calls for trim, stair details, built-ins, ceiling treatments, or the finishing stage of a remodel.

Based in Kewaskum, the approach stays personal and straightforward: show up, pay attention to the details, and leave behind work that still looks right years later for clients across Washington, Waukesha, Ozaukee, and nearby counties.
